Features

Picture to Speech

A great choice for the nonverbal community, this picture communication device serves as a great replacement to the picture exchange communication book. Create your own speech items with custom photos and speak with just the click of a button!

Type to Speak

Type your message in the Type to Speak feature to speak your mind! Just click the "Speak" button to read out your thoughts.

Speech to Text

Have trouble typing? Just speak into the microphone and the Speech to Text feature will convert your words into text.

About

My Voice is a passion project inspired by my two siblings with autism, one of whom is nonverbal. My family and I have seen firsthand how crucial communication devices can be in new and unfamiliar situations for members of the autism community who have trouble expressing themselves. For years, my sister has used a picture exchange communication book (pictured) given to her in school to communicate. However, this is limited and only has the absolute basics. My Voice's Picture to Speech feature provides customizable pictures and sayings that is easy to use and edit on the go.

A product like My Voice is something I have looked for for years, especially one with a customizable aspect that is also affordable. The goal of My Voice is to create an affordable communication device that is easy to use and meets you where you are, helping you communicate at any level.
