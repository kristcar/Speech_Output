Welcome to My Voice!

No matter your need, My Voice will meet you where you are. With three communication options, My Voice is the right choice for you.

Choose between Type to Speak, Speech to Text and Picture to Speech options.

Features

Picture to Speech

A great choice for the nonverbal community, this picture communication device serves as a great replacement to the picture exchange communication binder. Create your own speech items with photos and speak with just the click of a button!

Type to Speak

Type your message in the Type to Speak feature to speak your mind! Just click the "Speak" button to read out your thoughts.

Speech to Text

Have trouble typing? Use the Speech to Text feature to convert spoken words into text. You can even edit in place as you type to make sure you capture everything said.
